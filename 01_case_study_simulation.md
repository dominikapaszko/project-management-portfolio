# Case Study: Online Payments Feature in Mobile App

## Context and background
- Mid-size company specialized in car financing and leasing sector  
- Mobile application for clients to manage contracts, download invoices, contact with customer service and view offers  
- Problem: clients could not pay invoices online, which led to low app adoption and dissatisfaction
- Initiative to update application came from the Product Owner and App Store user feedback

## Project Goal
- **Business Goal:** Increase app adoption, improve customer satisfaction, and strengthen competitiveness  
- **User Goal:** Allow clients to make fast, safe and intuitive payments directly in the app  
- **Success Metrics:**
  - % of payments made through the mobile app  
  - Number of active app users  
  - App Store rating

## My Role in the Project
- Responsible for end-to-end delivery of the new payment functionality  
- Defined project scope and aligned requirements with Product Owner and stakeholders  
- Planned project timeline and coordinated cross-functional team  
- Managed project risks and dependencies  
- Coordinated testing, acceptance, and release activities  

**Stakeholders:**
- Mobile Development Team  
- Product Owner  
- QA  
- Compliance  
- UX  
- Accounting

## Project Scope

**In Scope:**
- Enable online payments for invoices directly in the mobile app  
- Integration with online payment system  
- Update payment status in the app

**Out of Scope:**
- Advance payments for future invoices  
- Other payment methods outside the app

**Dependencies / Constraints:**
- Correct accounting of payments in the bank system  
- Sending payment confirmation emails to clients  
- Synchronization with the accounting system

## Project Plan & Timeline
The project was executed using **Agile methodology**, divided into four main stages:

1. **Requirements & Planning**  
   - Define project requirements, assumptions, budget, and timeline  
   - Stakeholders: Project Manager, Product Owner

2. **Legal & Compliance Check**  
   - Verify legal and regulatory constraints  
   - Stakeholders: Compliance team, Legal advisors

3. **Implementation & Testing**  
   - Prepare development environment, implement functionality, conduct testing  
   - Stakeholders: Analysts, IT/Development team

4. **Deployment & Communication**  
   - Release the new feature in the mobile app and notify clients  
   - Stakeholders: Marketing, Customer Support

## Risks & Mitigation

**Identified Risks:**
- Payment errors or failed transactions  
- Incorrect posting of payments in the accounting system  
- Delays in invoice issuance  
- Legal or regulatory constraints (including GDPR)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Conduct thorough testing of payment functionality before release  
- Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements  
- Present project plan to the steering committee and gather feedback  
- Align closely with accounting and compliance teams to verify processes

## Results & Lessons Learned

**Results:**
- 70% of new leasing clients chose online payments  
- 45% of existing clients started using the mobile app for payments  
- App Store rating increased from 3,9 to 4,3

**Lessons Learned:**
- Collect feedback from all stakeholders; different roles identify different opportunities and constraints  
- Transparent communication across teams is essential to align expectations and deliverables  
- Close collaboration with compliance, IT, and finance is critical for successful deployment
